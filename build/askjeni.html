Meet Jeni Burkhart

Our student loan coach

Jeni graduated in 2013 with $132,000 of student loan debt despite working two jobs and going to a public in-state university. After making an expensive mistake during repayment she decided people shouldn't have to make expensive mistakes while repaying their loans and dedicates Jeni is committed to relentlessly pursuing student loan debt solutions so that borrowers can make smart financial choices with clear, upbeat, and powerful information.

Jeni has written a book Repayable that walks readers through the best way to repay their student loans based on their financial situation. Additionally, she blogs at www.repayable.org She believes the student loan debt crunch can be fixed and is starting the movement by empowering borrowers to repay their loans.
